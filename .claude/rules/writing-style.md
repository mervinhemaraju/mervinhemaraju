# Writing Style

## Em dashes

Never use the em dash character "—" anywhere: not in docs, comments, commit messages, or any other written output.

Replace it with:
- A colon `:` when introducing or explaining something ("boots normally: red LED on")
- A semicolon `;` when joining two related clauses ("not set at 5V; verify before use")
- Parentheses `()` for asides ("the Pi (set it aside until step 8)")
- A plain hyphen ` - ` when a light separator is needed in prose
- A full stop `.` when two sentences are cleaner than one long one
